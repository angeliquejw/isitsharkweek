Shark Week Aquarium Events

  • California
    • Shark Week at Birch Aquarium at Scripps is July 17-23 and will feature live shark encounters, shark-focused dive shows, shark scavenger hunts, and a "Great White" photo booth.
  • Florida
    • Enjoy a behind-the-scenes shark feeding tour during Shark Week--or any Tuesday and Sunday--at The Florida Aquarium. The Aquarium will also be running Shark Week discounts, so check their Twitter account.
  • New Jersey
    • Adventure Aquarium in Camden will be celebrating Shark Week July 31-August 8. Visit their 30+ sharks up close and in person and walk through the 40-foot suspended shark tunnel. Wear a shark fin on your head and get $2 off the price of admission!
    • Jenkinson's Aquarium will have different shark activities every night of Shark Week.
  • North Carolina
    • The Aquarium at Pine Knoll Shores will have a variety of shark-themed activities and special exhibits July 25-31.
  • South Carolina
    • Shark Week at the South Carolina Aquarium is July 30-August 8 and will feature interactive dive shows, hands-on shark learning, Shark Week Silly Bandz, and a viewing of Jaws on the Aquarium's lawn!
